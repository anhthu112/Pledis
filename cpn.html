WHO ARE WE

PLEDIS innovates the music industry business model under the mission, "We believe in music". PLEDIS strive for the world's best entertainment lifestyle platform company based on music. "Content" that lead the global trend and "fans" who are our customers are the two pillars to our values, and we are committed to satisfying our customers by continuously improving our services with the highest standards.
